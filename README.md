# Computer Science Student Support Platform

## Overview
Developed a platform for Computer Science students to connect with resource persons for academic support, while allowing experts to find students to share their knowledge.

## Features
- **Student Support:** Enables students to seek guidance from experienced professionals.
- **Expert Networking:** Helps experts connect with students and share their expertise.
- **Seamless Communication:** Facilitates direct interaction between students and mentors.
- **Resource Sharing:** Allows the exchange of study materials and resources.

## Purpose
This platform aims to bridge the gap between students and experts, fostering a collaborative learning environment for academic and professional growth
